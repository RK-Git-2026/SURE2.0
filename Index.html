How trust is built in

The school sees the pattern. The teacher keeps the detail.

A lot of observation tools end up feeling like surveillance, which is why teachers quietly resist them. Suraasa Sure works the other way around. The same recording gives the school and the teacher two different things, and keeping those two views separate is what makes the trust real.

For the school
Patterns across the school.

Leaders see where teaching is strong across the school and where it needs more support. The view is aggregated, so it shows trends across the staff rather than a ranking of individual teachers.

For the teacher
Detail that stays with you.

Each teacher's insights, scores and coaching notes are their own. They decide what those mean, what to work on, and what they want to share with anyone else.

That separation is what we mean by trust

How it works

It all runs from a single recording.

1

Record

A teacher records a lesson on their phone. That recording is the only thing Sure ever needs from anyone.

2

Insight report

The session becomes a Class Insight Report covering strengths, useful signals and the teacher's One Thing for that lesson.

3

Personal coaching

The teacher's AI coach shares cards built around their own classroom, plus a chat that's open whenever they need it.

4

Leadership view

The same information rolls up into anonymized patterns for school leaders, unless a teacher chooses to share more themselves.

It runs on Suraasa's CPAT framework. And if something wasn't in the recording, nobody gets asked to supply it.

Your classroom, your growth, your data.
